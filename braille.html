1. Braille Signages

Braille is a specialized tactile writing system developed for people with visual impairments. The raised dots embossed on these signs create recognizable characters, that help the users comprehend the text by feeling the dots.

Nowadays, braille language is increasingly being used and made part of way finding and informational signs installed in Hospitals, Government Buildings, Police Stations, Museums, Railway Stations and Airports etc.

Our braille signs are developed in full compliance with the guidelines laid down by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). We use sophisticated automated manufacturing processes that ensure high level of accuracy and precision.

Used Materials:

Flexible PVC, PU, or LDPE plastic with high-intensity reflective tape.

Braille Handrail Signs

Braille Handrail signs are commonly installed on staircase handrails and ramps to help visually impaired users of essential way-finding information such as climbing directions, floor locations, sudden turns, warnings about obstructions or any other information as the situation demands. These informational messages are embossed with braille on the inside of the handrail so that they can be easily read by the visually impaired.

Used Materials:

Flexible PVC, PU, or LDPE plastic with high-intensity reflective tape.

Differently-Abled W/C Alarm System

This Differently-abled Alarm system is available as a single zone kit which can be expanded up to 3 zones. It has been designed for differently-abled persons/old persons in case of emergency/panic. It meets the requirements of BS8300.

Key Features: Wireless operations, supports up to 3 zones, LED indication, auto shutdown function, and includes a Differently-abled W/C Braille Sign.

Specs: 23A 12V Battery, 20-100m Range, DC 12V/230V AC, 433.92 MHz frequency, 100m transmission range, ABS/Acrylic Dome body.

Used Materials:

High-quality ABS/Acrylic Dome construction with wireless transmission components and LED indicators.
