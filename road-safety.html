Road Safety

Comprehensive road safety solutions for all your needs

Our Expertise

We offer a wide range of road safety products to reduce accidents and improve traffic flow. Our solutions include speed breakers, reflectors, bollards, cones, and barricades – all built with durable, weather-resistant materials. Designed to meet safety norms, our products are suitable for highways, city roads, and industrial zones. We ensure quick delivery and professional installation.

Traffic Cone

Traffic Cone

Traffic cones, also called road cones, highway cones, safety cones or construction cones, are cone-shaped markers that are placed on roads or footpaths to temporarily redirect traffic in a safe manner. They are often used to create separation or merge lanes during road construction projects or automobile accidents. Traffic cones help to mandate increased night-time visibility via the placement of additional reflective white bands on cones. Traffic cones are compatible with message plates, plastic chain, cautionary barricading tape etc. Traffic cones are available in different sizes and shapes.

Used Materials:

Flexible PVC, PU, or LDPE plastic with high-intensity reflective tape.

Traffic Barricades / Expandable Barriers

Traffic Barricades / Expandable Barriers

Traffic Barricades/Barriers are portable in nature and designed in a way that makes barricading more effective, safe and more stable than drums or other traditional types of barricades. These environment-friendly barricades are recyclable and made out of HQ plastic materials. They are visually more substantial and command the respect of drivers. The 'Linear design' of these barricades helps in making a positive visual separation between vehicles, bicycles and pedestrians. With the ability of stacking them, it helps saving up to 80% in transportation, storage and handling costs.

Used Materials:

High-density polyethylene (HDPE) or ABS plastic with reflective sheeting and interlocking metal/plastic joints.

Speed Bumps & Rumblers

Speed Bumps & Rumblers

A speed bump / rumbler are a velocity-reducing feature of road design to slow traffic or reduce through traffic. A speed bump/rumbler is a bump in a roadway with heights typically ranging between 1 to 3 inches (25mm to 75mm). Speed bumps are useful in areas where vehicle speeds are statutorily mandated to be low. They are built using high-quality rubber and ABS plastic material to help last longer. They are very useful for various spots on the Highways, Parking lots, Toll-plazas, Shopping malls, Hotels etc.

Used Materials:

Vulcanized rubber or recycled plastic with embedded glass reflectors and reflective paint.

Portable Speed Bump

Portable Speed Bump

Portable speed bumps are made of rigid & durable plastic having rubberised carpet base. It is fitted with reflective tapes for long distance visibility. They are suitable for Emergencies, Events, VIP movements, Traffic Check points, Toll Collection sites, and other temporary traffic control applications.

Used Materials:

Lightweight rubber or polyurethane with reflective strips and interlocking connectors.

Delineators

Delineators

Delineators are designed to give the best reflective performance to show the road geometry during day and night making it convenient for drivers to keep them away from road hazards. This helps in avoiding accidents. Delineators are particularly beneficial at locations where the alignment might be confusing or unexpected, such as at lane reduction transitions and curves. Delineators are effective guidance devices at night and during adverse weather. An important advantage of delineators in certain locations is that they remain visible when the roadway is wet or snow covered. They can also be used on long continuous sections of highway or through short stretches where there are changes in horizontal alignment. These are available in various designs and sizes.

Materials: PU or ABS plastic with reflective prismatic sheeting

Blinkers/Road Lamps

Blinkers/Road Lamps

Portable Blinkers can be used for traffic control, military operation, parking guides, airports, emergency security, construction jobs and many others. All they help in controlling traffic during night time and provide signals to the motorists from far off distances.

Materials: Polycarbonate or ABS housing with LED lamps and optional solar panel

Cat Eyes / Pavement Markers / Road Studs

Cat Eyes / Pavement Markers / Road Studs

Pavement markers are useful for various purposes. From internal city/town roads to highways, industrial complexes to special economic zones, residential/commercial townships to office complexes and from shopping malls to parking plazas; pavement markers are indeed very useful and successful for application in these areas. They are available in different sizes/colours.

Materials: ABS, aluminum, or ceramic body with glass bead or prismatic reflectors

Solar Studs / Solar Delineators

Solar Studs / Solar Delineators

Solar delineators and Solar Cat Eyes (also called road studs) carry LED mini-lamps to allow increased battery life and help them last longer. They are designed to improve the road safety level especially in places where the weather conditions negatively impact the driving visibility.

Materials: Aluminum alloy or polycarbonate with LED lights, solar panel, and rechargeable battery

Spring Post

Spring Post

Spring posts are designed with specially developed materials which enhance the strength & resistant against crashes and shocks. They are extremely flexible and soft which helps preventing any damage to the vehicle at collision. Moreover, its unique feature allows it to restore back to its original shape after collision. Its one piece construction allows for greater durability. These spring posts are UV stabilized & have a non-fading colour. Spring posts are great for centre-lane dividing for narrow roads, multi-lanes for broad roads, sharp curves, NO or LIMITED ENTRY zones, Dangerous medians, Dangerous U Turn areas and many others.

Materials: UV-stabilized flexible PU with high-intensity reflective tape

Centre Verge

Centre Verge

Centre Verge is made out of ABS (Astronitrilite Butadiene Styrene) & PC (Poly–Carbonate) alloy, UV Stabilized material and special design with extra ribs at the bottom gives more strength to it. Built in space is given to mount highly flexible spring post made out of PU (Poly–Urethane) which is not only visible during day time due to its fluorescent orange color but in night time also as it has two bands of highly reflective tape.

Materials: ABS and polycarbonate alloy body with PU spring post and reflective sheeting

Linear Delineation System (LDS)

Linear Delineation System (LDS)

LDS is made with bright, durable Prismatic Grade sheeting technology with unique crimped wave pattern which provides superior visibility—even at high entrance angles. LDS is available in various colors. It is easy to install & is a low-cost safety device which helps to avoid accidents on roundabouts and medians.

Materials: Prismatic reflective sheeting with flexible plastic backing

Aluminium Backed Flexible Prismatic Tape (AFP)

Aluminium Backed Flexible Prismatic Tape (AFP)

AFP is made of non-metallic flexible prismatic retro-reflective sheeting having uniform black colored arrows/slant line pattern. It is 50 micron thick aluminium foil laminated at back, pressure sensitive adhesive and a easily removable liner at the back of the aluminium foil. It is suitable for uneven surfaces like trees concrete poles, culverts etc., that hamper road safety.

Materials: Prismatic reflective film with 50-micron aluminum foil and pressure-sensitive adhesive

Reflectors/Conspicuity Tapes

Reflectors/Conspicuity Tapes

We have a wide range of reflectors and reflective tapes, which are used for traffic safety purposes and are available in bright colours. Made from the finest quality materials, these reflectors and reflective tapes are available in various shapes and sizes. These are highly durable and weather resistant. Conspicuity Tapes are meant for contour marking applications, confirming to the AIS-090 standards & tested and approved by Automotive Research Association of India (ARAI), Pune.

Materials: Retro-reflective sheeting, PSA adhesive, and acrylic or polycarbonate lens

Caution Tape

Caution Tape

Caution Tape (also known as warning tape or police tape) is resilient plastic tape of a signal colour or highly contrasting colour combination (such as yellow-black or red-white).

Materials: LDPE or PVC film with UV-resistant printed ink

Median Markers

Median Markers

Median markers are made of tough, impact resistant thermoplastic body, reflectorised on either sides using fluorescent yellow prismatic sheeting. Median Markers are highly durable reflective product for medians. Designed to take the rigors of natural weathering, this product make the medians extremely visible thereby preventing accidents and saving invaluable lives on roads.

Materials: Impact-resistant thermoplastic with fluorescent yellow prismatic sheeting

Car Stopper/Parking Lot Solutions/Rubber Wall Protector

Car Stopper/Parking Lot Solutions/Rubber Wall Protector

Car Stoppers are made from high quality rubber and are highly durable, weather resistant, shock absorbent and maintenance free. These are used in parking areas to prevent damage to the walls and also to guide the drivers while parking. These are available in various sizes and colours.

Materials: Recycled rubber or high-density polyethylene (HDPE)

Guard Rail

Guard Rail

Guard rails are made from high quality steel and are used to prevent vehicles from colliding with dangerous obstacles such as steep slopes, sharp curves, or other hazards. They are designed to redirect vehicles back onto the roadway and are an essential safety feature on highways and other high-speed roads.

Materials: Galvanized steel or weathering steel beams with reflective markers

Anti-Glare

Anti-Glare

Anti-glare screens are designed to reduce the glare from oncoming headlights, especially during night driving. They help improve visibility and reduce driver fatigue, making night-time driving safer. These are particularly useful on highways and other roads with heavy traffic.
