Company Overview

Anand Sales (INDIA), established in 1985, is a leading provider of total safety solutions in India, trusted by national and multinational clients for over four decades. Our SAFE ZONE brand is synonymous with quality, innovation, and reliability, offering a comprehensive range of products for traffic, industrial, indoor, and public safety. As an authorized converter of Avery Dennison, we deliver world-class reflective signage and safety systems, backed by 7–12 years of warranty and strict quality assurance in our 10,000 sq. ft. facility. We are proud to support the “Accessible India Campaign” with our advanced accessibility products, designed to create barrier-free environments for people with disabilities and the elderly. At Anand Sales (INDIA), we are committed to customer satisfaction, continuous improvement, and making India safer, one solution at a time.
