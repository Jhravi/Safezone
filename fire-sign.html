Fire Signages

Fire signs are critical safety indicators used to show the location of fire extinguishers, alarms, hose reels, emergency exits, and fire assembly points. They provide quick visual guidance during fire emergencies and help ensure safe evacuation. Fire signs are mandatory in all public, commercial, and industrial premises to comply with fire safety regulations.

Materials & Features:

Made from glow-in-dark vinyl, aluminum sheets, acrylic panels, and weatherproof laminated boards.
