Company Overview

Anand Sales (INDIA) is one of the leading total safety solutions provider with a wide range of safety solutions ranging from traffic to indoor safety and from industrial to public safety. Incorporated in 1985, Anand Sales (INDIA) has been the #1 choice of national and multi-national clients operating in India. Our brand, SAFE ZONE has proven its ability to consistently provide products that meet customer and applicable regulatory requirements.

We are thrilled to have added another feather to our cap by launching a revolutionary line of accessibility products. Committed to creating a barrier-free environment for people with disabilities and the elderly. Our new offerings seamlessly align with the objectives of the “Accessible India Campaign” – an initiative by the Government of India. Our manufactured products are in line with National Building Code of India.

We are authorized converter of Avery Dennison, the global leader and manufacturer of pressure-sensitive reflective materials, which has enhanced our quality standards, thus, enabling us to provide high-quality signage systems to a developing nation like India. We aim to enhance customer satisfaction through effective application of the latest technology system and backing our in-house manufactured signs with 7–12 years of warranty.

With 10,000 square feet of industrial space, all our safety equipment is closely inspected and checked for quality assurance to enable us to deliver high-quality products that match world-class standards.
