Vehicle-Graphics

Comprehensive vehicle-graphics for all your needs

Our Expertise

We are a trusted manufacturer of high-quality vehicle graphics, offering custom-designed vinyl wraps and decals for branding, advertising, and styling purposes. Our graphics are ideal for fleet vehicles, delivery vans, commercial taxis, and promotional cars. Made from UV-resistant and weatherproof materials, they are durable, easy to apply, and remove without damaging the vehicle’s surface. We provide reliable solutions for both custom designs and bulk orders.

Reflective Vehicle Tapes

Reflective vehicle tapes are used to enhance vehicle visibility during night or low-light conditions. They help prevent accidents by reflecting headlights, making vehicles noticeable from a distance. Commonly used on commercial, emergency, and public transport vehicles.

Materials Used:

Made from microprismatic or glass bead retro-reflective vinyl with pressure-sensitive adhesive backing.

Vehicle-Graphics

Vehicle graphics are decorative or informative designs applied to vehicle surfaces for branding, advertising, or styling. These can be full wraps, partial decals, or logos, used widely by companies for mobile advertising.

Materials Used:

Made using high-quality UV-resistant vinyl sheets with weatherproof adhesive.

Revolving Lights, Siren & Strobe Lights

These lights and sirens are used on emergency or priority vehicles like police, fire, and ambulances to alert and signal their presence. Strobe lights provide high-visibility flashing patterns for urgent identification and safety.

Materials Used:

Constructed with polycarbonate lens, LED/halogen bulbs, and ABS or aluminum housing.
