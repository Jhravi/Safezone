Indoor Sign

High-quality Indoor sign for all your safety needs

Our Expertise

We provide high-quality indoor signage solutions that enhance wayfinding, branding, and interior aesthetics. Designed for clarity and visibility, our signs are perfect for offices, hospitals, hotels, and commercial spaces. With durable materials and professional finishes, they ensure lasting impact and easy installation.

Floor Directions

Floor direction signs are essential for effective wayfinding in large buildings such as malls, hospitals, airports, and offices. They guide visitors toward exits, elevators, restrooms, and departments, minimizing confusion. These signs improve navigation flow and reduce the need for staff assistance. Floor directions can be customized with arrows, symbols, or text based on specific layouts.

Materials Used:

Anti-skid self-adhesive vinyl, laminated directional stickers, or acrylic panels with UV printing.

Hospital Signs

Hospital signs play a vital role in maintaining order and clarity in healthcare environments. These include signs for emergency areas, OPDs, ICUs, wards, consultation rooms, and more. They ensure patients, visitors, and staff can quickly locate necessary departments or services. Clear and well-placed signage is essential for safety, efficiency, and a stress-free experience.

Key Features:

Durable materials, clear typography, and standardized color coding for easy recognition.

Inauguration Signs

Inauguration signs are used during formal ceremonies to highlight important names, titles, and event details. These signs lend a dignified and celebratory touch to openings of buildings, institutions, or events. Designed with elegant finishes, they often include engraved or printed text with logos and decorative borders. They are widely used by government bodies, corporations, and NGOs.

Materials Used:

Acrylic, metal, wood, or PVC with premium finishing options like gold/silver foiling or laser engraving.

Safety Signs

Safety signs are crucial for preventing accidents and ensuring workplace safety. They include warning signs, mandatory signs, prohibition signs, and emergency information signs. These signs help in hazard identification and ensure compliance with safety regulations. They are commonly used in industrial facilities, construction sites, and public areas to communicate important safety messages effectively.

Key Features:

High-visibility colors, durable materials, and clear symbols for quick recognition and compliance with safety standards.

Q-Managers & Classic Posts with Velvet Ropes

Q-Managers and classic posts with velvet ropes are essential for crowd control and queue management in various settings. These are commonly used in banks, airports, retail stores, and event venues to organize customer flow efficiently. The velvet ropes add a touch of elegance while maintaining order. They are adjustable, easy to install, and can be customized with different finishes to match the decor.

Materials Used:

Stainless steel, brass, or powder-coated metal with premium velvet or polyester ropes.

Name Plates

Name plates are used for identification and branding purposes in offices, corporate buildings, and public spaces. They display names, designations, and other relevant information in a professional manner. Available in various materials and finishes, they can be customized to match the interior decor. Name plates enhance the professional appearance of workspaces and help in easy identification.

Materials Used:

Acrylic, metal, wood, glass, or PVC with options for engraving, printing, or embossing.
