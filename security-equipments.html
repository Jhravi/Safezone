Security-Equipments

High-quality Security-equipments for all your safety needs

Our Expertise

We offer a wide range of security equipments designed to meet modern safety needs. From handheld detectors to advanced surveillance systems, each product ensures reliable performance and durability. Ideal for offices, public places, and industrial setups, our solutions help maintain a secure and controlled environment.

Handheld Metal Detectors

Portable Security Devices

Handheld metal detectors are ideal for use at airports, banks, public events, police, industries, and other sensitive areas to detect any metal items carried by individuals. The product range includes both handheld and walk-through devices. These portable security devices provide reliable detection while being easy to use and maintain, making them essential for security screening in various environments.

Materials & Features:

High-sensitivity sensors, ABS plastic housing, rechargeable battery systems, adjustable sensitivity, audio/visual alerts, lightweight design

Door Frame Metal Detectors

Walk-Through Detectors

These detectors are ideal for high-security areas and are designed to accurately detect concealed metal objects as individuals pass through. They provide efficient and non-intrusive screening at airports, government buildings, events, and industrial facilities. Our door frame metal detectors combine advanced technology with robust construction to deliver reliable performance in high-traffic environments.

Materials & Construction:

Stainless steel frames, advanced digital signal processors, multi-zone detection coils, weatherproof housing, adjustable sensitivity, audio/visual alerts

Search Light LED/Halogen

Portable Lighting Solutions

Hand-held search lights are microcontroller-based lighting devices. These lights come with an inbuilt SMPS switcher that includes a switching regulator to deliver the required DC output voltage. The integrated handle enhances ergonomics and usability. A unique feature allows users to reduce the intensity of the light, making it ideal for searching at short distances. Perfect for security personnel, emergency services, and industrial applications.

Materials & Features:

Polycarbonate or aluminum body, high-intensity LED/halogen bulbs, inbuilt SMPS, ergonomic handle, adjustable intensity, rechargeable battery

Traffic Convex Mirror

Safety Mirrors

Traffic convex mirrors assist drivers while crossing indoor/outdoor driveways in multi-level parking lots, basement parking facilities, shopping malls, and other locations. These mirrors provide a wide-angle view of blind spots, helping to prevent accidents and improve overall traffic safety. Available in various sizes (450mm, 600mm, 800mm, 1000mm) to suit different applications and visibility requirements.

Materials & Specifications:

Unbreakable polycarbonate or acrylic lens, fiber-reinforced plastic (FRP) backing, UV-resistant coating, rust-proof steel mounting brackets, available in 450/600/800/1000mm sizes

Automatic Traffic Boom Barrier

Vehicle Access Control

Automatic traffic boom barriers are equipped with reliable technology to suit the specific needs of the client. These barriers feature electronic slow-down at the end of travel during both opening and closing, ensuring smooth and safe operation. The internal drive mechanism is designed to deliver durable and consistent performance with minimal maintenance. Ideal for parking lots, toll booths, and secure facilities requiring controlled vehicle access.

Construction & Features:

Powder-coated steel or aluminum body, electronic control panel, geared motor system, counterweight arms, automatic/manual operation, safety sensors, LED indicators
