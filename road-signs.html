Road Signs

Comprehensive road signs solutions for all your needs

Our Expertise

Road Signs are essential for safe and efficient traffic management. They provide clear, standardized guidance to drivers and pedestrians, ensuring smooth movement and reduced accidents. Our signs are manufactured with high-grade reflective materials, ensuring maximum visibility in all weather conditions. We offer a wide range of regulatory, warning, and informational signs tailored to meet national standards and client-specific needs.

Highway / Road Signs

Highway / Road Signs

Highway signs are large-format, high-visibility signage systems specifically designed for national and state highways to provide critical information, regulations, and guidance to fast-moving traffic. These signs include category-specific formats such as directional boards, route numbers, distance markers, and large overhead gantries. Their primary purpose is to ensure smooth, safe, and well-regulated vehicular flow on long-distance routes. These signs must adhere to IRC and MoRTH standards and are often required in infrastructure projects under NHAI or PWD. Properly installed highway signage significantly reduces confusion, enhances safety, and ensures driver compliance at high speeds and complex junctions.

Used Materials:

Made using powder-coated aluminium or galvanized iron sheets mounted on MS pipes or gantries with high-intensity prismatic reflective sheeting (ASTM/IRC compliant).s

Retro-Reflective Road Signs

Retro-Reflective Road Signs

Retro-reflective road signs are manufactured using advanced reflective sheeting that enhances visibility by reflecting headlights, especially at night or in low-light conditions. These signs are essential for highway and urban road safety as they guide drivers effectively and help prevent accidents. Their durability and high visibility make them a key requirement in all government and infrastructure road projects. As per IRC:67 and MoRTH (Ministry of Road Transport & Highways) guidelines, retro-reflective signage is mandatory on all public roads.

Used Materials:

Made from aluminium sheets or galvanized iron with high-intensity or micro-prismatic retro-reflective sheeting (as per ASTM/IRC standards)

Cautionary Signs

Cautionary Signs

Cautionary signs are triangular traffic signs designed to warn drivers about potential hazards or changes in road conditions ahead, such as sharp curves, pedestrian crossings, or steep slopes. These signs are crucial for alerting road users in advance, allowing them to slow down or take necessary precautions. By reducing the risk of accidents, cautionary signs play a vital role in road safety. Their installation is mandated by the Indian Roads Congress (IRC) and local traffic authorities for all major roads and highways.

Used Materials:

Vulcanized rubber or recycled plastic with embedded glass reflectors and reflective paint.

Mandatory Signs

Mandatory Signs

Mandatory signs are circular blue signs with white symbols that indicate actions that road users must follow. These signs provide instructions that must be obeyed, such as direction of travel, minimum speed limits, or requirements to wear safety gear. They are essential for maintaining order and safety on roads by clearly communicating non-negotiable rules to all road users. Their use is standardized under IRC and MoRTH regulations to ensure consistency across India's road network.

Used Materials:

Aluminium or galvanized steel sheets with high-intensity reflective sheeting, mounted on MS pipes or frames.

Informatory Signs

Informatory Signs

Informatory signs provide useful information to road users about destinations, facilities, and points of interest. These rectangular blue or green signs help drivers navigate unfamiliar areas by indicating distances to cities, locations of services like hospitals and fuel stations, and other important landmarks. They are crucial for efficient traffic flow and reducing driver stress. These signs must comply with IRC specifications for size, color, and placement to ensure maximum readability and effectiveness.

Used Materials:

High-grade aluminium composite panels with weather-resistant digital or screen printing and retro-reflective coatings.

Railway Signs

Railway Signs

Railway signs are specialized traffic control devices installed at railway crossings and along railway tracks to ensure safety and provide critical information to both motorists and pedestrians. These signs warn of approaching trains, indicate crossing distances, and provide instructions for safe crossing procedures. They are essential for preventing accidents at railway crossings and along tracks. These signs must meet strict railway safety standards and are typically installed in coordination with railway authorities.

Used Materials:

Heavy-duty galvanized steel or aluminium with high-intensity reflective sheeting, designed for extreme weather conditions.

Airport Signs

Airport Signs

Airport signs are specialized wayfinding and regulatory signs used in and around airport premises to guide passengers, visitors, and ground staff. These include runway signs, taxiway signs, terminal direction signs, and safety signs that help maintain order and efficiency in the complex airport environment. They play a crucial role in ensuring safe aircraft movement and smooth passenger flow. Airport signs must comply with international aviation standards (ICAO, FAA) and local aviation authority regulations.

Used Materials:

Aircraft-grade aluminium, stainless steel, or composite materials with photoluminescent or LED-illuminated surfaces.

Photoluminescent Signs

Photoluminescent Signs

Photoluminescent signs are safety signs that absorb and store ambient light, then emit it in dark conditions without electricity. These self-illuminating signs are crucial for emergency egress paths, stairwells, and exit routes in buildings, providing continuous visibility during power outages or smoke-filled conditions. They are increasingly required in commercial buildings, hospitals, and public spaces as part of fire safety regulations. These signs offer a reliable, maintenance-free solution that remains visible when traditional lighting fails.

Used Materials:

Photoluminescent vinyl or paint applied to various substrates like aluminium, acrylic, or PVC, compliant with ISO 16069 and NFPA 101 standards.

Exit / Emergency Light Signs

Exit / Emergency Light Signs

Exit and emergency light signs are critical safety devices that provide illuminated wayfinding during emergencies, power failures, or low-visibility conditions. These signs combine high-visibility graphics with internal illumination to mark exit routes, emergency exits, and safety equipment locations. They are essential for quick and safe building evacuation during emergencies. Required by building codes and fire safety regulations, these signs must maintain illumination for a minimum duration during power outages and are tested to strict safety standards.

Used Materials:

Durable acrylic or polycarbonate face with LED or fluorescent illumination, backed by ABS plastic or metal housing with battery backup systems.
